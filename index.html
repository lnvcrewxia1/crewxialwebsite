About CrewXIAL

CrewXIAL, founded by Industry experts under the guidance of Director Industry Partnership & CSED Mohd Talha Khan sir and Dr. Akash Sanghai sir (HOD Academic Block 3), is a student-driven initiative that empowers passionate learners to gain recognition through innovation and real-world exposure. We organize hackathons, seminars, webinars, and on-campus events to bridge the gap between academics and industry expertise.

What We Do

At CrewXIAL, we curate, conduct and participate in innovative hackathons, webinars, conferences, and networking events that connect academia with industry. Focused on cybersecurity and emerging technologies, our platform fuels innovation, learning, and career growth for aspiring tech enthusiasts.

Past Successful Events

CrewXIAL has actively participated in some of the most recognized hackathons and tech events across the country. Our journey includes contributions to the Hack2Skill Innovation Hackathon, Gemini CLI Home API Hackathon, Geekverse Hackathon, GDG Campus Solutions, and the Invertis Techathon. We also proudly represented ourselves at the Convergence India Expo 2025, reflecting our commitment to innovation, collaboration, and real-world impact in the fields of cybersecurity and emerging technologies.

Volunteer Program

Introduction

Join our dynamic volunteer program and become an integral part of CrewXIAl's tech community! Our volunteer program offers students and tech enthusiasts the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in event management, network with industry professionals, and develop valuable skills while contributing to exciting tech events.

Benefits

  • Exclusive access to all CrewXIAl events and workshops
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Certificate of participation and letter of recommendation
  • Develop leadership and organizational skills
  • Priority consideration for internships and job opportunities
  • Free swag and merchandise from our partners
  • Behind-the-scenes experience in tech event management

Roles & Responsibilities

As a CrewXIAl volunteer, you'll be involved in various aspects of event planning and execution. Responsibilities may include event setup, registration management, speaker coordination, social media promotion, technical support, and participant engagement. Volunteers are expected to commit to at least 2 events per semester and attend mandatory training sessions.

Ethical Guidelines

All volunteers must adhere to our code of conduct which includes maintaining professionalism, respecting diversity and inclusion, protecting confidential information, avoiding conflicts of interest, and representing CrewXIAl positively at all times. Violations may result in termination from the volunteer program.
